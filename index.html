💼 Project Section – Expanded Content

🧱 AI-Based Fly Ash Brick Model:
This Final Year Project focuses on integrating Artificial Intelligence into sustainable construction. Using a dataset of physical and mechanical properties of fly ash bricks, we developed a machine learning model that predicts compressive strength and durability. The project not only promotes eco-friendly construction but also reduces time and cost in material testing.

🌊 WASA Drainage Planning Internship:
During my internship at WASA Lahore, I worked under XEN Muzaffar Abbas, contributing to the design and evaluation of urban drainage systems. I learned practical application of flow routing, pipe sizing, and GIS-based mapping, helping the authority in stormwater management planning.

🚰 PRMSC Public Health Internship:
I assisted in overseeing water supply and sewerage systems under a World Bank-funded public health project. I observed real-time construction site supervision of STPs (Sewerage Treatment Plants), pipeline trenching, and chlorination protocols in rural Punjab under the Tehsil Manager.

📐 Estimation Internship (USA Projects):
Worked on US-based residential and commercial projects using PlanSwift software for takeoff and quantity estimation in CSI Divisions 03 (Concrete), 22 (Plumbing), 23 (HVAC), and 26 (Electrical). Gained hands-on experience in blueprint analysis, bid preparation, and estimator workflows.
